Got innovative ideas.Try them all out!
Innovation is border less and universal.
A free event with exclusive prizes is anticipating your arrival.

Deadline for Online Registration: 25th March 2019

General Guidelines:

  • The registration can be made both online as well as offline(On the Spot).
  • Students have to carry their own laptops with android studio installed in it.
  • The software that is to be used for application development is Android Studio
  • Themes will be given on the spot.
  • Internet facility will not be provided to the participants.
  • Participants will be given 6 hours to develop their ideas in an application.
  • Teams would also have to create PowerPoint presentations supporting their project Idea.
  • Winners will be awarded with cash prizes.
  • The decision taken by the judges would be final.
  • Entry through valid college i-cards.

    • The event will comprise of 2 rounds:
    Round 1: A common theme will be provided to all the contestants and they will be given time to formulate an idea to develop an app.
    Round 2: The teams will be given score for their idea submission and then they will be allowed to work on their projects. The best innovative as well as creative ideas and its implementation will win you amazing prizes.


Team Size :-
Should be strictly 3 to 4.

Do you have what it takes to solve problems ?
Do you have the power to think, create and execute ?
In short are you ready for the rough ride ?
Then codecoaster is definitely for you.

Deadline for Online Registration: 25th March 2019

General Guidelines:

  • There will be both online and offline(on the spot) registrations.
  • Winners will be awarded with cash prizes.
  • Entry through valid college i-cards.

    • The event will consist of 3 rounds:
    Round 1: Find the code(prelims) - It will consist 20 questions to be solved in 20 minutes.
    Round 2: Switchroo (mains)- This round will be of 1 hour 15 min ,both the team members will be provided with two questions each which they have to solve on different computer. In every 15 minutes the team mates will be switched and they have to work on their partner's code.
    Round 3: Go bonus (will be revealed on the spot).


Team Size :-
Should be strictly 2.
